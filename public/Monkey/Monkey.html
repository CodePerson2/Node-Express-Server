How To Play
Controls for desktop: "A" to move left, "D" to move right, "SPACE" to jump and double jump, Mouse left click to throw banana.

Controls for Mobile: Joystick to move left and right, Jump Button to jump and double jump, Throw button to throw banana

Game begins after atleast 2 players are in lobby and everyone is "Ready"

Objective of game is to have the banana for the shortest amount of time.

Once any player gets a score of 60 the game ends.
